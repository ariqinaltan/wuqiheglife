Photos: Kim Kardashian Reveals Her Natural Hair

Kim Kardashian is certainly a pro at grabbing everyone's attention, from flaunting her gorgeous shapes to sharing her hot and sexy bikini photos.

The model, fashion designer, and reality television star has been very influential with hair trends and this time she decided to ditch the extensions and reveal her natural hair, which fell just below her shoulders, during a whirlwind trip to Paris with her fiance Kanye West.

Kim's natural hair in a bun

When Kim arrived in Paris her hair was pulled back into a small bun, which she later released in the car to reveal the more natural look.
