Gun disguised as marker: Police seize gun disguised as marker, WA

A man has been charged after a homemade gun disguised as a marker pen was seized from a Perth home.

During a search of a Seville Grove property on September 20, police seized a homemade operable pen gun manufactured to look like an Artline marker.

An electronic stun device, knuckle dusters, ammunition, drugs and cash were also seized during the search.

A 25-year-old man – who is also allegedly a Gypsy Joker outlaw motorcycle gang member – was charged with multiple offences, including possession of a prohibited drug, prohibited weapon and firearms.

He is next due to appear in the Armadale Magistrates Court on October 3.

On Monday, detectives searched a home in Bassendean with alleged links to the Lone Wolf outlaw motorcycle gang.

During the search officers located and seized a homemade machine gun.
