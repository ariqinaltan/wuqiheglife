Man Caught Texting Liv Morgan Impostor By Girlfriend of 8-Years

Liv Morgan is undoubtedly one of the most beloved female WWE Superstars in the company right now. She is out of action due to injury and she is still on her journey to recovery so far. Unfortunately, a fake account posing as her might have just damaged an 8-year relationship.

The advent of social media is a boon as well as a curse, as it has let fans interact with their favorite WWE Superstars even more now. The drawback is the fact that there are many impersonators as well.

As seen in a video making rounds on social media, a couple was approached and asked to put their trust on the line by exchanging their phones. While the woman was safe, the man got caught texting a fake Liv Morgan account.

Not only that, but he kept on texting the fake account – which ended up hurting his partner in the process and possibly hampering their 8-year relationship. It was truly painful to watch for fans.

Liv Morgan’s WWE return remains in limbo for the time being as her injury persists. Morgan is also getting back into shape as she is determined to return to the ring as soon as possible. We’ll have to see when she will be able to make her in-ring comeback, as fans sorely miss her.
