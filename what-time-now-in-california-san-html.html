West Coast Living

Ah, San Francisco. The land of sourdough bread, cable cars, and...wait for it...actual sunshine (sometimes). Whether you're cruising down Lombard Street in a flower-filled VW bug or chowing down on a fog-drenched Dungeness crab Cioppino, there's a certain mystique to this city. And hey, maybe you're catching this at a more reasonable hour – in which case, enjoy that perfect Californian weather!

Timezone Trivia (optional)

Did you know? San Francisco is in Pacific Standard Time (PST) during winter and Pacific Daylight Time (PDT) during summer. Fun fact for your next trivia night!
