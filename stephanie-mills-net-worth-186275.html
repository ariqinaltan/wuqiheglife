Stephanie Mills Net Worth

Stephanie Mills is a Grammy Award-winning R&B singer and actress from Queens, NY, born on March 22, 1957. She is best known for her role as Dorothy in The Wiz on Broadway, and for her hit single "Never Knew Love Like This Before," which peaked at #6 on the Billboard Top 100.
