FAQs

Does Venus offer free shipping?

Unfortunately, Venus doesn't have a free shipping option available at this time. However, their standard shipping option is discounted to $7.95 when you spend over $100. Their express and next-day shipping options are also discounted when your order is over $100. Those signed up for Venus Insider are eligible for free shipping when their orders are over $75. 

How often does Venus have sales?

Venus has seasonal sales and random sales events throughout the year. However, the website also features a dedicated sales section where you can browse discounted products in both specific categories or explore the entire stock. This section is updated regularly and is definitely worth a peek for those looking to save a little money. Another way to save money is by using one of our Venus coupon codes.

Does Venus have a newsletter discount?

Venus has both an email-based newsletter as well as a text alerts program to help customers stay in the loop on upcoming sales and special events. These subscriptions also provide customers with exclusive Venus discount codes, deals, coupons, and more to help save money on their purchases. Signing up for the newsletter is free and you can always opt out at any time.

Is there a Venus Clothing Plus Size range?

Yes, there is a Venus Clothing Plus Size section on their website. You can find a range of stylish clothing in sizes up to 3XL, meaning that women can feel comfortable and fashionable no matter their size. 

Does Venus Offer Free Returns?

Venus has a return policy that permits customers to exchange or return items for a refund. Anything sent to Venus for a refund will include a $6.95 return fee that is deducted from the refund price. Exchanges are free, however, as well as choosing to receive Venus Wallet Credit instead of a cash refund. Items must be returned within 90 days of the purchase and should be in unworn condition. Certain products on the website are labeled as “Final Sale Item. No exchanges or refunds”. These products are not eligible for refunds or exchanges at all.
