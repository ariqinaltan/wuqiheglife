Demi Moores Chic Winter Outfit Recipe Is Shockingly Easy to Recreate

Demi Moore hasn’t just perfected the cool-girl look; she’s perfected effortlessly chic winter dressing. Last week, the actress stepped out in a timeless black duster coat, wide-leg jeans, booties, and a vintage tee. The outfit was the right mix of casual-cool pieces and timeless elements — a combination that worked so well, she used it again this week. 

While out in New York, Moore used a number of those same ingredients to create a look that felt effortless but still sophisticated. On top, she reached for that same winter jacket, which features an oversized fit and collar, and on the bottom, she opted for slouchy blue jeans with a casual raw hem and heeled black ankle boots. Her repeat-look proved that when you’ve perfected a recipe, you really don’t need to deviate from it. Fortunately for me — someone who’s obsessed with everything about her winter ‘fit — it’s shockingly easy to replicate. 

If you’re wanting to achieve her casual-chic, three-part winter look, I recreated it for $234.
