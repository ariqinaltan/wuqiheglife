Hulk Hogans Daughter Brooke Is Ready For Christmas With Skimpy Bikini Photo Drop

Brooke Hogan is the daughter of WWE Hall of Famer Hulk Hogan. Despite being the daughter of one of the most famous wrestlers of all time, Brooke managed to stay out of the wrestling business for most of her life. She only appeared for brief stints with the WWE and Impact Wrestling (fka TNA).

Although she was involved in some key storylines on TNA, her tenure with the company didn’t last very long. Following her departure from TNA, she pursued a career in acting and singing. She even released her single “Touch My Body” in 2019.

Recently, Brook Hogan took to Twitter to show that she is in a Christmas mood with a skimpy bikini photo drop. She captioned the picture as follows:

“Ready to start the Christmas countdown…”
