Erica Mena Claims She Isn't A Fan Of Nicki Minaj But Old Tweets Prove Otherwise

Erica Mena and her man Safaree paid a visit to Hot 97 with Nessa to talk about all things surrounding their relationship and of course, the topic of Safaree's ex Nicki Minaj came up. Erica was asked if she is a fan on Nicki and if she bumps the rapper's music. “Honestly I grew up on Lil Kim so heavy, that when [Nicki Minaj] came out, I was like ‘ugh she’s biting my girl.’ It just was what it was,” she explained, where Nessa let her know that “Nicki’s still great.” 

“She is, I’ve just never been drawn to it,” Erica responded. “It was always just cartoon-y and stuff. She’s great at what she does, but me personally…if it’s in the club and you’re nice. But I would never…I don’t know the words.”

Naturally, The Barbz took the statement into their own hands and did some digging that proves Erica may have told some lies. Old tweets from Erica's Twitter feed prove that she actually was a fan of Nicki at one time. The Love & Hip Hop New York star once tweeted Nicki's lyrics and even tweeted at Nicki, dissing Lil Kim at the same time.
