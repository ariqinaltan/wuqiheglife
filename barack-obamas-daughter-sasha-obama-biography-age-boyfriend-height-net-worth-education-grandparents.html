Early Life & Education

Sasha Obama, the younger daughter of Barack Obama, the 44th president of the United States, and Michelle Obama, was born on June 10, 2001, in Chicago, Illinois. Alongside her older sister, Malia Ann Obama, Sasha grew up in a politically influential family. 

She grew up alongside her siblings and grandparents, Marian Shields Robinson, Stanley Ann Dunham, Barack Obama Sr., and Fraser C. Robinson III.

She received her elementary and secondary education at Sidwell Friends School in Washington, D.C., from 2009 to 2019. Sasha Obama continued higher education after high school and earned an undergraduate degree from the famous University of Southern California (USC) in May 2023.

Career

Following her father’s presidential tenure, Sasha Obama remained in Washington, D.C., where she diversified her talents and pursuits. During her sophomore year, she assumed the role of captain of her school’s cheerleading squad, gracing the cover of American Cheerleader in 2018. 

Beyond cheerleading, Sasha Obama excelled in volleyball, securing three District of Columbia State Athletic Association (DCSAA) tournament titles and being crowned prom queen in her senior year. She debuted in high society at the International Debutante Ball in New York City.

In Michelle Obama‘s memoir Becoming, it’s revealed that Sasha Obama‘s transition from basketball to cheerleading was initially met with her father’s disappointment, yet he remained supportive of her performances. Sasha‘s cheerleading journey peaked as the point flyer for Senior Elite, part of the Cheer Extreme Allstars from 2013 to 2019, winning gold at the Cheerleading Worlds in 2016 and 2018.

After graduation, Sasha Obama attended the University of Michigan for two years before joining the NFL’s Los Angeles Rams Cheerleaders. Her crowning cheerleading moment was performing at Super Bowl LVI at SoFi Stadium in Inglewood, California, where the Rams emerged victorious on February 13, 2022. Sasha now resides in Los Angeles, earning a Bachelor of Fine Arts from the University of Southern California.

Sasha Obama‘s professional path is still evolving, but her commitment to social justice remains steadfast. She aspires to a career as an artist, citing Hannah Montana as an influence. Beyond cheerleading, she’s displayed her talents in tennis and basketball. Sasha also gained work experience at Nancy’s, a renowned seafood restaurant in Martha’s Vineyard, Massachusetts, in August 2016.
