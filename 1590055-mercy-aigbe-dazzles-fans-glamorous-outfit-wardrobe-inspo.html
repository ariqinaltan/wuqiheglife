Mercy Aigbe Dazzles Fans With Polka Dot Outfit, Gives Wardrobe Inspiration: &quot;Queen of Fashion&quot;

  • Actress Mercy Aigbe-Adeoti looked fabulous as she wore a beautiful dress for her goddaughter's wedding
  • The lovely polka dot dress has extravagant fabric behind it and was designed with fur at the hem of the sleeves
  • Her outfit was made to perfection, cinching her waist and pushing up her bosoms to expose some cleavage

Nollywood actress Mercy Aigbe-Adeoti showed her unending love for fashion as she wore a polka dot dress for her goddaughter's wedding.

The top was stylishly designed with gold beads. A red star was hand-beaded on the gold, adding more glamour to the attire. The sleeves were not regular, as the designer added some glamour to them. It was puffed at the shoulders and had fur at the hem.

She added a gold 'gele', which was beautifully tied and matched her dress.
