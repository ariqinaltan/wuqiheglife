Conor McGregor Imposter Causes A Scene In New York City

Islam Badurgov, a street workout athlete from Kazakhstan, has the build, haircut and beard that might trick some unsuspecting people into believing he's Conor McGregor. 

Last month, Badurgov invaded Southern California equipped with a lavish Bentley and McGregor's signature style, right down to his unique tattoos, and had everyone completely fooled as he galavanted around SoCal, posing for pictures with fans and causing a scene of hysteria everywhere he went.

The McGregor imposter recently pulled off a similar prank on the East Coast, causing a scene at Times Square in New York City as he once again donned his Conor McGregor disguise, right down to his cauliflower ear.

Of course, the real McGregor doesn't have time to be parading around NYC all day, his much anticipated boxing match against Floyd Mayweather is taking place just a few weeks from now at the T-Mobile Arena in Las Vegas on August 26th.
