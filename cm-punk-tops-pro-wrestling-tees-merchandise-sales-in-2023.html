CM Punk Tops Pro Wrestling Tees Merchandise Sales in 2023

CM Punk’s return to WWE at Survivor Series last year has made him a significant draw, and his popularity has been steadily growing each day. Fans continue to get more excited every time they see him. Now, he has topped the Pro Wrestling Tees’ merchandise sales for last year.

The Second City Saint has been on a roll ever since he came back to his former company after a decade, as fans are still trying to process just what he has been able to do in such a short span of time.

Prior to his success in WWE, CM Punk was also one of the biggest stars in AEW even though he was highly controversial there. After all, he was still one of the company’s biggest merchandise sellers.

Pro Wrestling Tees took to their official Instagram page and listed the top 25 sellers of 2023. CM Punk would top the list, followed by Jay Briscoe and MJF. Dan Hausen and Kenny Omega would complete the top 5.

‘‘Happy New Year! Check out the 2023 PWT Top 25 Selling Wrestlers. This includes all merchandise sold on PWTees & ShopAEW. We wish Jay were here to see his photo at the top of this list” 

CM Punk also joined an exclusive clothing brand recently. CM Punk will be competing in the Royal Rumble match on January 27th, so we will have to wait and see if he can win the match this month.
