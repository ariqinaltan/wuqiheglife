Can I heat brie cheese in the microwave?

Answer

Microwave for 1 minute 15 seconds on high, or until brie is very soft when prodded in the center. When you microwave the brie, keep an eye on it; if it puffs up too much (risking exploding), turn the microwave off and leave the brie inside; residual heat will continue to melt it.

Is brie perishable?

Cheese may go bad at any time, but some decay considerably faster than others. Brie has a short shelf life since it is a soft cheese, and it is only excellent for one or two weeks after it has been opened when properly stored in the refrigerator.
