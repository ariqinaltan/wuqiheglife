50 Cent Took Down A Snitch In Front Of J. Lo

Producer Cory Rooney recently gave fans a blast from the past, sharing an incident involving 50 Cent. He told BagFuel an old story of 50 Cent getting into it with a man he believed snitched on his friend, Prince Markie Dee, for straying from his girlfriend. Rooney has frequently worked alongside J. Lo and signed 50 Cent in the '90s.

Rooney begins, “We’re at the Hit Factory, and I’m with Jennifer in the studio. 50 shows up that day, and he got the dude with him. So he comes to me and he’s like, ‘Yo, where Mark at?’ I said, ‘I don’t know.’ He says, ‘Call him on the phone.’ So, I put him on the phone, 50’s like, ‘Ay yo listen, I got this n***a here right now, so wassup, tell me what happened.'”

50 Cent Stuck Up For Prince Markie Dee

Rooney continued the story, "The dude was looking around, and he put Mark on speaker, and the dude was saying, ‘Nah, that’s not what happened.’ So after awhile, 50 was looking at him and says he’ll call Mark back. And then, 50 starts pounding this dude, and Jennifer is like, ‘Oh my God.’ She started screaming. Then I told him, ‘Take the n***a outside.’”

Earlier this week, 50 Cent took to Instagram to share his thoughts on Bill Cosby's recent lawsuit from former Playboy bunny, Victoria Valentino. Valentino alleges in her lawsuit that Cosby drugged her and a friend in the late '60s, leading to a nonconsensual sexual experience. 50 Cent commented publicly, “They are just doing Bill dirty, smh ain’t no way 54 years ago. Lmao this is just fvcked up Brody!” Cosby and his legal team deny the allegations. The rapper also recently revealed that his highly-anticipated upcoming tour could potentially be his last. He called the tour a  "final lap," adding, “I won’t be running around like this no more. I gotta do my film and television stuff and got a lot of other things going on behind the scenes that I gotta work on. So I won’t be out touring as much as I’ve been touring… I’m having fun this time because it’s set up to enjoy myself, we not feeling pressure.”
