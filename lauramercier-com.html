FAQs

How can I get 15% off at Laura Mercier?

Simply sign up for newsletter emails or text updates to get a 15% off Laura Mercier discount code. You can go to their website, scroll down to the website footer, and click ‘Sign Up’. Once you type your preferred email address, Laura Mercier will send your unique code. 

Does Laura Mercier have free shipping?

Laura Mercier has free shipping on orders of at least $35. This is standard US shipping and should arrive within 5-8 working days. If your order is less than $35, shipping costs $5.95. Check this page for free shipping coupon codes as we sometimes will get these from the brand.  

Can I get student discount at Laura Mercier?

Yes. Students can get 20% off Laura Mercier with UNiDAYS. You will need to verify your student status by signing into UNiDAYs. Once you’ve verified your discount, you should be able to get your Laura Mercier coupon code. 

When will Laura Mercier have sales?

Laura Mercier has sales all year on their dedicated ‘Sales’ page. Simply go to the Laura Mercier website, hover over ‘Make Up’, and click ‘Sale’. We’ve seen discounts of up to 30% off in the Laura Mercier sales before. Be sure to shop their Black Friday and Cyber Monday sales too, for some of their best savings. 

What is the Laura Mercier returns policy?

Laura Mercier has a 30-day return policy. The cosmetic brand states you can return any ‘gently used’ products by this time. Your items will need to be bought directly from lauramercier.com. The refund will be made to your original payment method. For a list of exclusions, go to the Laura Mercier ‘Returns’ page.
