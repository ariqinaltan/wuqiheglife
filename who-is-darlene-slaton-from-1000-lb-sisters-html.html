Who is Darlene Slaton from 1000-Lb Sisters?

Spread the love             

As the third season of “1000-Lb Sisters” unfolds, fans are diving deeper into the lives of the Slaton sisters, Tammy and Amy. Their courageous journey towards weight loss surgery has captivated viewers, with Amy making strides while Tammy faces her own challenges. Amidst all the drama, the spotlight has also fallen on Darlene Slaton, the mother of the Slaton siblings. In this article, we’ll explore more about Darlene, including her background, relationships, and her appearances on the reality show.

Meet Darlene Slaton

Darlene Slaton plays a significant role in the lives of Tammy and Amy Slaton as their mother. However, their relationship hasn’t always been smooth sailing, as revealed on “1000-Lb Sisters.” The sisters have openly discussed their upbringing, acknowledging that they didn’t receive the attention and affection they craved. It’s important to note that while they believe their mother cared, expressing love wasn’t her strong suit. Their perception is that Darlene struggled to convey her emotions, possibly influenced by her family’s upbringing.Darlene was born on December 25, 1960, which means she will be celebrating her 61st birthday on Christmas Day. Originally from Shawnee town, Illinois, she now resides in Kentucky.

Marital Status and Family

Darlene’s marital history is somewhat enigmatic. Her first husband remains unknown to the public, but together, they had five children: Amy, Tammy, Amanda, Mistry, and Chris. The reasons behind their divorce are shrouded in mystery.In 2013, Darlene tied the knot with Robert Franklin. Tragically, Robert passed away in January 2021, marking a somber chapter in Darlene’s life. The couple shared two children, Crystal and Wayne.Currently, Darlene is single, and it’s unclear whether she has entered into a new relationship following her late husband’s passing.

Viewers’ Reactions to Darlene Slaton

Given her frequent appearances on “1000-Lb Sisters,” Darlene has naturally become a topic of discussion on social media platforms. Viewers have not been shy about sharing their thoughts on her character.Some have drawn connections between Tammy’s demeanor and her mother’s, suggesting that they share certain traits. One Twitter user remarked, “Dear lord. Darlene is a piece of work,” while another questioned, “Does Darlene like ANYTHING?”Interestingly, a memorable scene during a Mother’s Day afternoon tea in a recent episode had fans in fits of laughter. Amy’s humorous interactions, including an encounter with a honey bee, left viewers amused. One viewer humorously commented, “Omg this whole scene of them at high tea and Amy cussing out a honey bee has me about to pee my pants.”While Darlene’s portrayal on the show may evoke varied reactions, there’s no denying her presence has added depth to the Slaton sisters’ journey, making “1000-Lb Sisters” all the more captivating for its audience.
