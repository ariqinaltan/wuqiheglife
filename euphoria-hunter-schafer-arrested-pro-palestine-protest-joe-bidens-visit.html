'Euphoria' Star Hunter Schafer Arrested at Pro-Palestine Protest

Euphoria star Hunter Schafer was among a group of protestors who were taken into police custody on Monday. The protest was organized by the activist organization Jewish Voices for Peace. It was planned to disrupt President Joe Biden’s appearance on Late Night with Seth Meyers at NBC television headquarters in New York City and to call on Biden to “…stop supporting the Israeli government’s genocide against Palestinians in Gaza.” – Jewish Voices for Peace NYC.
