On May 9, 2023, Shireta Rogers, the wife of gospel musician and pastor Tim Rogers, left this world unexpectedly. The news was shared through a heartfelt Facebook post where Pastor Tim expressed gratitude for the prayers and love received during this challenging time.

At only 46 years old, Shireta’s departure has left many questions unanswered as the cause of her death remains undisclosed. The post also included a link to Pastor Tim’s cash app for those willing to contribute in support of the grieving family.

A Beacon of Light:

Shireta and Pastor Tim were a shining light in their community, living their lives in service to their faith and uplifting those around them. Shireta, like her husband, was deeply devoted to her faith and music, using her talents to mentor new vocalists and contributing significantly to the success of “Tim Rogers & The Fellas.”

In January 2023, the couple marked their 25th wedding anniversary, blessed with seven children over the years. Shireta’s legacy extends beyond her immediate family, reaching those she inspired through her role as a songwriter, a mother, and an educated musician.

Remembering Shireta’s Impact:

Shireta’s influence reached far and wide, leaving an indelible mark on the lives of those she touched. Despite the sorrow that has gripped the church community in Blytheville, Arkansas, her memory lives on through the love of music she shared and the guidance she provided to aspiring vocalists.

Pastor Tim Rogers, known for his dedication to his faith and community, comes from a lineage of clergy. Following in his father’s footsteps, he began preaching at the age of 18 and ventured into music in the early 2000s, forming “Tim Rogers & The Fellas.”

A Message of Hope:

Community Support for Pastor Tim:

Since the news of Shireta’s passing, an outpouring of condolences and tributes has flooded social media. The community, deeply moved by Shireta’s influence, stands in support of Pastor Tim and his family during this difficult time.

Facing the Future:
