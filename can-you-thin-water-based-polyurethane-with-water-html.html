Can you thin water based polyurethane with water?

Answer

Thin the poly with water if it’s water-based and too thick (no more than 10 percent). If you reside in a state that permits it, you may also thin solvent-based poly by adding up to 10% mineral spirits.

Is it possible to thin water-based varnish using water?

Water-based coatings may be thinned with water to increase atomization, although the process differs from lacquer thinning. A little amount of water makes a considerably larger effect in lacquer than lacquer thinner. So start with 5% or less and see how things go.
