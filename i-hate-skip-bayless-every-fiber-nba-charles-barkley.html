NBA legend Charles Barkley is not a fan of Fox's Sports Skip Bayless and has let it be known to the world. 

The NBA Hall of Famer turned sports media icon sounded off on Bayless for his comments about Tom Brady and Bill Belichick during an appearance on a popular nationwide show.

On Friday, Barkley,60, stopped by the Dan Patrick Show to discuss the NBA season and the league's All-Star weekend.

However, Barkley quickly took the conversation in another direction towards the NFL and took a shot at Bayless for his "asinine" comments about Brady, Belichick, and the New England Patriots dynasty. 

It's unclear what comments Barkley is referring to, but Bayless is on record stating on his Fox Sports show 'Undisputed' that Belichick is overrated since Brady left.

"I think Bill Belichick has gotten exposed without Tom Brady," Bayless said midway through the NFL regular season.
