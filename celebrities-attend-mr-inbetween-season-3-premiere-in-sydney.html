Mr Inbetween season 3 premiere at Sydneys Circular Quay sees Scott Ryan, Brooke Satchwell attend

One of Australia’s most popular TV dramas is coming to an end with its hugely-anticipated final season set to air in days.

Celebrities attended the season three premiere of Foxtel and FX series Mr Inbetween at United Cinemas at Sydney’s Circular Quay on Wednesday night, with creator, writer and lead actor Scott Ryan leading the arrivals.

The award-winning Aussie actor was joined by the show’s director Nash Edgerton, as well as his fellow Mr Inbetween cast members including Chika Yasamura (who is also Edgerton’s daughter), Jeremy Sims, Damon Herriman and Brooke Satchwell.

The black comedy series, which was filmed in Sydney late last year and will wrap up with nine episodes, is immensely popular in the US, with The Hollywood Reporter dubbing it “one of 2018’s best shows” for its first season debut, while the New York Times included it on their Best of Fall 2018 TV list.

The praise continued when the second season aired in 2019, with Rolling Stone writing: “The huge improvement from an already solid first season to this tremendous second one has me wondering if Mr. Inbetween has another big leap in it.”

The third and final season will continue to follow charismatic hit man Ray Shoesmith (Ryan), in a role which earned him an Best Lead AACTA in a TV Drama and a TV Week Logie Award for Most Outstanding Actor in 2019.

The final season also sees the return of Justin Rosniak as Ray’s best friend Gary, Chika Yasumura as daughter Brittany, Damon Herriman as Freddy, Matt Nable as Dave and new guest cast members Jeremy Sims and Emily Barclay.

The series is written by Ryan and directed by Edgerton.

Ryan and Edgerton also serve as Executive Producers with Michele Bennett as Producer.

Mr Inbetween Season 3, episodes 1 & 2 premiere Wednesday May 26 at noon on Fox Showcase
