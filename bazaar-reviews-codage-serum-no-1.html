Bazaar Reviews: Codage Serum No 1

What they say: Codage (French for code) has created a wardrobe of serums. There is one for each and every skin type and concern. The chic brand blends science with nature to create potent, results-driven formulas.  

What we say: Blurring the line between oil and a serum, Codage’s No 1 has the most incredible texture and melts into skin. It’s the first time I’ve been happy to forgo a cream and use two serums and an SPF instead.

Usability: Two drops will cover your face and neck. The texture lends itself well to massaging the formula into the skin, unlike some serums which can tend to roll.

Results: Skin feels moisturised and velvety all day, even in the areas like the cheeks, which usually dry out throughout the day.  

Insider tip: Don’t stick to the same products day-in day-out. Mix them up depending on how your skin feels each day.

Codage Serum No1, £64, www.selfridges.com
