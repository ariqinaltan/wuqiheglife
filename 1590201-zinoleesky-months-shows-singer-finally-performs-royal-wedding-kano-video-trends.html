Zinoleesky: After Months of No Shows, Singer Finally Performs at Royal Wedding in Kano, Video Trends

  • Nigerian singer Zinoleesky trended online as he graced his first event since the demise of his colleague and former label mate Mohbad
  • Recall that a good number of Nigerians called for the cancellation of the Marlian signee after he was accused of bullying the late Ask About Me crooner
  • A new video sighted by Legit.ng saw Zinoleesky in a royal gathering as he entertained them with his vocals, igniting a fresh round of criticism 

Fast-rising Nigerian singer Oniyide Azeez, aka Zinoleesky, buzzed the internet following his first appearance since the death of his colleague, Mohbad.

Recall that the Marlian signee was alleged to have bullied the departed artist alongside his boss, Naira Marley and his associate, Sam Larry.

This led several Nigerians to clamour for his cancellation from the scenes.
