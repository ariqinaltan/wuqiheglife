Iskra has millions of followers on Instagram and is a top model for Aerie, an American lingerie retailer that centers all of its launch campaigns around her to promote healthy body image for girls. The model opposes photo shopping or any kind of image enhancement through software or surgery as it promotes authenticity and reality. The #AerieReal campaign refuses to remove any body imperfections, tattoos or cellulite on any ad.

Lawrence inspires young girls and women from around the world; She receives hundreds of emails and comments from girls around the world who have benefited from her fitness and nutrition classes. Iskra follows a strict fitness diary and follows the motto: Everything in moderation. The model shares her food diary online with stylecaster.com .

Iskra, a passionate maniac, revealed that she does a lot of squats to stay fit and pointed out how she hates the term plus size for its negative connotations as it affects the self-image of women in this category. Curvy, talented and inspirational personality is definitely someone you will love to meet.

Iskra Lawrence Bio (Age)

Iskra Arabella Lawrence was born on September 11,1990 in Wolverhampton, a town in the West Midlands of England. Her name Iskra, which means “Spark” in Russian and Polish, suggests Eastern European descent.

She has a British father named Doug Lawrence and an Irish mother whose name is not given. She attended Malvern St James, an independent girls’ school, and Bromsgrove School, an independent public school in Worcestershire. From there she went to the National Youth Theater at the age of 15. She later learned to swim and competed at the national level when she was 15 years old.

She signed a contract with ELLE fashion magazine at the age of 13, however the contract was canceled when she was 16 when she suddenly began to gain weight in the hips and her self-esteem took a nose dive, throwing the young model into a battle with an eating disorder and body dysmorphia.

Determined to make her mark in the industry, young Iskra carved a niche for herself and her type, continuing to distribute her pictures to agents. She was spotted in 2017 by American Eagle Outfitters (American Eagle), who portrayed her services as one of the top models for the New American Jean L’Oreal Paris unedited images of her models for cosmetics giants.

The voluptuous model has also been named a brand ambassador for the National Eating Disorders Association (NEDA) and a pioneer of the NEDA Inspires Award. She refuses to allow any kind of enhancements to Instagram images and is very vocal in criticizing people who promote the weight stigma placed on individuals. Iskra featured in the 2017 Straight Curve documentary as a presenter on the show The Pursuit of Perfection Quitting the same year.

She was named among BBC World’s 100 Women and was elected to the World Association of Women Leaders and Guides in 2017. Moreso, she gained more recognition as a brand ambassador of Persona collection campaign in 2018 for a major womenswear brand Marina Rinaldi as well as L’Oreal Princes Trust Ambassador.

In addition, she was the creator, producer and host of The Mirror Challenge with Iskra, a Facebook web series from the Clevver Media stable.
