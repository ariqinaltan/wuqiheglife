Hulk Hogan Allegedly Confronted Fan With A Shotgun For Showing Up At His House

WWE Hall of Famer Hulk Hogan is one of the biggest and most influential names in the pro wrestling business and has accomplished a lot in the industry. However, it appears Hogan allegedly confronted a fan with a shotgun for showing up at his house.

Hulk Hogan is a legendary figure in the world of professional wrestling, with his name being synonymous with the sport. While he has done a lot of questionable things over the years, none of that has erased his importance to the business.

While speaking on The Wrestling Outlaws, former WWE Superstar EC3 told a story of how Hulk Hogan once allegedly confronted one of his family members with a shotgun for showing up at his house.

“A black sheep part of my family, apparently, that I don’t talk to, we’re in Clearwater. And they got Hogan’s address, and somebody told them, ‘Yeah, he’s cool. If you just drive up to his house, he’ll take a picture with you.”

“I just found this out, and this is many years ago. But, like, they’re white trash. But they drove up to Hogan’s house. The gate was open because I think somebody was detailing his car, so the gate was open. They drove out, got out, they’re looking around, and Hogan comes out with a shotgun, going, ‘Who are you, and why are you here?’ He’s like, ‘You can’t come everywhere!’ And he eventually took a picture with him.”

Hogan also believes he should have ended his pro wrestling career around the 20-year mark. Regardless, we will have to wait and see what’s next in store for the Hulkster, as he has a lot to look forward to in life.
