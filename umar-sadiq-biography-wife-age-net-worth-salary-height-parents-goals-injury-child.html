Biography

Umar Sadiq Mesbah (born 2 February 1997), widely recognized as Umar Sadiq, is a Nigerian professional footballer renowned for his prowess as a striker. He has garnered domestically and internationally acclaim, having previously represented the Nigeria national under-23 team and played a pivotal role in securing a bronze medal at the 2016 Summer Olympics.

Sadiq‘s journey in professional football has seen him showcase his talent across various clubs, including AS Roma, Glasgow Rangers, and Partizan. His exceptional skills have not gone unnoticed, earning him a spot in the Nigeria national team, where he has competed in prestigious international tournaments.

Currently, Umar Sadiq plies his trade for La Liga club Real Sociedad, further solidifying his reputation on the global football stage.
