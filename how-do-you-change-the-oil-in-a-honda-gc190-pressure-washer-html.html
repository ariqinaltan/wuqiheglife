How do you change the oil in a Honda gc190 pressure washer?

Answer

To replace the oil, first put a container underneath the engine, then remove the filler cap/dipstick, followed by the drain plug, which should be located exactly below and to the left of the oil fill hole, to complete the process. Once the oil has been drained, reattach the drain plug and fill the engine with oil until the level of the oil fill hole is reached.

Refer to the owner’s handbook for your pressure washer to find out where the plug is located. Remove both the oil drain stopper and the oil filler cap from the pump and allow the oil to flow out of the system. Replace the drain stopper with a new one.
