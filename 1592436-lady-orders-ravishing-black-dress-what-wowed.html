Lady Orders Ravishing Black Dress, What She Got Wowed Many: &quot;How Many Clothes I Wan Save Now?&quot;

  • A lady felt the need to rock a beautiful black dress she saw online, and she ordered it from her fashion designer
  • She asked her stylist to make the exact pattern, which had a keyhole opening in front and also at the back
  • The fashion designer delivered as promised, and the lady showed off the outfit on social media to the amazement of many

A lady caught the attention of many netizens after she displayed the classy black dress she ordered versus what she got.

The sleeve of the original outfit was designed with a transparent fabric, and the top had keyhole openings on the front and back. It was also stylishly designed at the waist area.

The fashion designer replicated the exact style, and every detail was followed. When the client wore the dress, she gave off the same vibe as the model from whom she got the dress inspiration.
