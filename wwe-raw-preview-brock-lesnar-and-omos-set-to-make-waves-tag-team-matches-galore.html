WWE RAW Preview: Brock Lesnar and Omos Set to Make Waves, Tag Team Matches Galore

WWE’s Monday Night RAW is all set to continue its Road to WrestleMania 39 as it airs live tonight from the Amica Mutual Pavilion in Providence, Rhode Island. The episode is expected to be a memorable one, with some exciting matches, including Brock Lesnar and Omos facing off to hype their WrestleMania 39 match, and Finn Balor and WWE Hall of Famer Edge meeting in the ring to settle their feud.

The event will also feature several other WWE Superstars, including Cody Rhodes, Seth Rollins, Bobby Lashley, WWE Women’s Tag Team Champion Becky Lynch, The Street Profits, Alexa Bliss, Damage CTRL, The Judgment Day, Candice LeRae, Baron Corbin, and WWE United States Champion Austin Theory, as advertised on the WWE Events website and the arena website.

As for the matches, the line-up includes Luke Gallows and Karl Anderson vs. Maximum Male Models, Damian Priest and Dominik Mysterio vs. Johnny Gargano and Dexter Lumis, Bronson Reed vs. Elias, and RAW Women’s Champion Bianca Belair vs. Chelsea Green in a non-title match.

Fans can’t wait to see Brock Lesnar and Omos square off in the ring and what will happen during the Edge and Finn Balor segment, where a Hell In a Cell stipulation is expected to be announced for their upcoming WrestleMania 39 match.

Stay tuned to Ringside News for more information about tonight’s RAW and live play-by-play coverage of the event starting at 8 pm ET.
