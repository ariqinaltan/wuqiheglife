Kylie Minogue has split from her fianc Joshua Sasse

    Kylie Minogue has split from her British actor fiancé Joshua Sasse. The 48-year-old singer is said to have called off the wedding over concerns about Joshua’s friendship with Spanish actress Marta Milans, and has asked him to leave the West London home they share. Kylie and Joshua, 29, first met in 2015 on the set of the US series , and announced their engagement in February 2016.

    The was first to report news of the split. A source told the newspaper: "She is absolutely devastated, totally heartbroken. She really believed he was The One. But she no longer trusts him." Joshua reportedly bonded with Marta last year during filming in Vancouver, Canada for the now-axed TV show . Kylie is said to have been upset by their closeness, and upon her return from Paris Haute Couture Fashion Week, asked Joshua to move out.
