Are there any Michelin star restaurants in Mexico?

Aug 11, 2024 Answer : There is no Michelin Guide for any Mexican city, so no, there isn’t one. This isn’t because there aren’t any worthy establishments; rather, there isn’t a guide because there isn’t one. New York, Chicago, Washington, DC, and San Francisco are the only cities in North America that have a guide available. Just to clarify, do Michelin-starred restaurants exist in Mexico? There are four Mexican restaurants that have received Michelin stars, despite the fact that there isn’t a Michelin guide for every country: Mexique in Chicago, Punto MX in Madrid, Casa Enrique in New York, and Hoja Santa in Barcelona, which is owned by Paco Méndez and Albert Adriá.
