Treasure Island Ada

Treasure Island Hotel & Resorts in Ada, Ghana is a beachfront resort that offers luxury accommodation and a range of facilities. The hotel is situated on the beautiful beach of Ada and features luxurious rooms with views of the Gulf of Guinea.

The hotel’s facilities include two restaurants, an outdoor swimming pool, and a spa for relaxation. Treasure Island Hotel & Resorts in Ada Foah offers a wonderfully unique setting for your Ghana vacation, retreat, or meetings.

Beautiful tropical vegetation, quiet streams, and classic art and architecture combine to create a breathtaking landmark in the heart of one of the most spectacular places on the planet, located near the Volta River and the Atlantic Ocean’s estuary.

An atmosphere of elegance and sophistication awaits you within our exquisite Treasure Island Hotel & Resorts in Ada, Ghana. From our beautiful water slides and unique architecture to our very comfortable guest rooms, 12D Cinema, Game Room, Horse Back Riding, Camel Riding, and so on, we provide the perfect vacation just steps away from the island’s thrills and excitement.

Which region is Treasure Island located in Ghana?

Treasure Island is located in Ada, Greater Accra Ghana.
