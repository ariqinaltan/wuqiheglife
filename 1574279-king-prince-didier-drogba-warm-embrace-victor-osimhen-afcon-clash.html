&quot;King and Prince&quot;: Video of Didier Drogba in Warm Embrace With Victor Osimhen Before AFCON Clash

  • A trending clip captured Ivorian legendary footballer Didier Drogba when he came across Nigerian striker Victor Osimhen during the AFCON 2023
  • The clip was recorded shortly before the Nigeria versus Ivory Coast encounter, which ended in favour of the Super Eagles
  • The two men warmly embraced each other in what social media users have termed a king-and-prince relationship

A video showed Nigerian striker Victor Osimhen in a warm embrace with legendary footballer Didier Drogba during the 2023 Africa Cup of Nations.

The video appears to have been recorded shortly before the clash between Nigeria and Ivory Coast.

The short video, which has gone viral, was shared by @beinsports_FR and later reposted by the Confederation of African Football (CAF).

The two men were happy to meet each other. They embraced each other warmly, with Drogba giving Osimhen a warm pat on the back before the striker walked onto the pitch.
