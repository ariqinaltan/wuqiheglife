Machine Gun Kelly’s journey to fame and fortune began in Houston, Texas, where he was born. His early life was marked by movement, as his family relocated often due to his parents’ missionary work. MGK’s rise to fame was not instantaneous. It took years of grinding and releasing music before he caught the public’s attention. From there, his career soared, with subsequent albums and singles topping charts and earning him a dedicated fan base.
